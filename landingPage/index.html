An alternative approach to discovering up-and-coming mobile applications

VYBE is a contemporary online destination for finding daily editorial content surrounding the cutting edge in mobile applications.

Currently the app store is a cluttered aggregation of applications with an emphasis on freemium games marketed towards children and promotion of apps that one could find on everyone and their mother's phone.

The majority of apps in today's landscape gain recognition through word of mouth, so we set out to bring you a curation of the latest apps based on your preferences for genre and location, presented in a minimalistic format, free of ads and paid promotions.
